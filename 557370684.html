Story

Bajillions is a fast and light trivia card game about gigantic numbers for 3-12 players! It teaches in seconds and plays in minutes. It's a light, snappy game perfect for starting or ending your game night!

There is a man named Clarence Russel. He is responsible for our facts. Our facts are complete, and so Clarence no longer works for us. There are rumors that he has access to a Secret Edition of Bajillions! with secret facts. These rumors are unfounded. Clarence Russel is not missed.

Bajillions! is a trivia party game for 3-12 players that is all about finding which number is bigger, and going with the crowd or going against it! Shuffle the deck of 100 facts and deal two out side by side. Then, everyone places bets on which fact has a bigger number. The numbers are then revealed and whichever number is bigger earns points for players that bet correctly! First to 15 points wins. 

Which is bigger?  The square footage of the world's biggest factory? Or the carrying capacity of the largest ship?

Know which is the bigger number? Then bet on it! Think one is much bigger? Then bet more to risk getting more points! Git points based on how right you are!

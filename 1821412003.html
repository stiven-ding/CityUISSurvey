Nano Starseeds Tarot: A Stellar Journey to Enlightenment

Hello! I am Welynne Rivera, a graphic designer/production artist, born and raised in Los Angeles, California. My career spans over 15 years in print and digital publishing. Currently, I am venturing into the world of illustration which has been a personal passion of mine for years.

It is exciting to announce that I have created my own set of "fortune telling" cards, called Nano Starseeds Tarot: A Stellar Journey to Enlightenment. It is an illustrated tarot deck of 78 full color 2.75" x 4.75" cards for you and your pets. The characters, Nano Starseeds, are very small, higher dimensional beings that travel through futuristic/innerworld landscapes accompanied by furry or feathered friends. The deck is divided into 22 Major and 56 Minor Arcana cards. The Major cards represent situations that are faced in the grand scheme of life. The Minor cards reflect the trials and tribulations that are experienced on a daily basis. By utilizing the "Nano" cards in readings, one can shed light on the pure and simple relationship between humans and animals. Do you ever wonder what your pet is truly thinking or feeling? Why he/she is behaving in a certain way? This is the Tarot deck for you! Each deck contains a Booklet of Interpretations to help unlock and answer key personal questions.

Project Downfall

   This is a Trading Card Game over 10 years in the making. I want to create a game that won't cost people a fortune and that is a lot of fun to play.

My plan

   The SEER and TITAN Deck Boxes were my first quality products made for gamers. The logical next step is a quality game. I want to develop launch an amazing experience. I plan on using our reputation in the community to get our products into OTS owners' hands. 

   The first regional event will be held shortly after in North Texas.

Rewards

   The rewards with this project are designed to make backers feel like a part of the game, which is the entire goal of the game to begin with. Being featured on a one of a kind card that you can actually play with is something rad that feels like something my fellow hobby gamers will love.

   Variant card art is another fun and valuable reward that I look forward to providing for eager backers.

Risks and challenges

The biggest risk to this project is getting the art done consistently. Fortunately, I have reliable artists ready to go. I am also ready to print with the same organization that produces the SEER and the TITAN.

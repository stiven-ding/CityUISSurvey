 About Dining Dynasties  

Dining Dynasties is a competitive game of building and running the most successful restaurant in town!  Players take on the role of aspiring restaurateurs, managing all aspects of their enterprise, providing a rich and realistic entrepreneurial experience.  

What makes Dining Dynasties unique is the way it blends familiar mechanisms to maximize player choices and avenues to victory.  Each game, players must choose the worker placement powers they wish, based on a restaurant floor plan they customize from 9 individual tiles.  Three unique card decks drive the action as players alternate between a focus on Marketing, Menu and Mood to climb ahead of the competition. 

 The Game  

 Gameplay Overview

 A Game of Dining Dynasties is played over 9 rounds, each with 5 key phases.   

The opening phase is the time to assign your workers, buy supplies for the coming round and possibly look to expand by adding a new room tile. 

Once preparations are complete, players now take actions to directly influence their restaurant's reputation and sales! 

In this phase, players may go head to head to determine who has the most popular new dish.  While this phase is optional each round, the extra investment in the Menu deck can pay off big with immediate bonuses. 

The phase where all your hard work can pay off with a roll of the customer dice pool.  How well you're managing your reputation and sales, as well leveraging your card abilities, will determine how much income you generate each round.  
 

This phase is the final step in which you pay your workers and clean up the cards you've played in preparation for the next round!     

Components 

4 Player Boards (1 for each player), 32 dice, 12 cubes (in 4 player colors), 40 meeples (in 4 player colors), 1 round tracking disc, 36 room tiles, 90 Coins (3 different values), 20 Finest Cuisine tokens, 1 First Player token, 1 rule book, 174 cards

 The Creators   

Majestic Moose Games is an independent game publisher located in Northeast Ohio (www.majesticmoosegames.com). The company founders come with a life long background of tabletop gaming and a rich history of experience in instructional design and client services, both highly relevant in today's hobby board game market.  With a focus on finding unique ways of blending mechanics and theme, the goal of Majestic Moose Games is to create experiences that are both fun and challenging, and above all, a reason to bring people back to the table.

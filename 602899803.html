Story

Two years ago, we ran a successful Kickstarter campaign for Custom 3D printed accessories for Scythe. The campaign allowed us to get a larger 3D printer, and to deliver some great accessories for Scythe by Stonemaier Games.

Now we're back! By continuing to hone our 3D modeling and printing skills, we designed what we believe to be the best storage solution for Stonemaier Games' newest game, Tapestry. The design for the inside of Tapestry does not allow a lot of room for all the player pieces in the vacuformed wells or above the board. We have worked hard through a lot of trial and error to design a compact, robust, storage solution that fits in these wells.

Each player gets their own organizer which holds all their buildings, cubes, towers, and resources. The organizers include outlines to help players know where to store pieces as well. A unique design allows these organizers to stack by slide-locking. Three of these organizers plus a lid just fits into each of the two wells.

The 6th organizer holds all of the Space hex tiles, and conveniently nests with the other organizers. Holes in the bottom allow you to easily push out tiles without damaging the sides.

The lid has a recessed area, making it easy to grip and slide off. The lids also allow for vertical storage without worrying about the pieces falling out.

In addition to the Player Organizers and Space Hex Organizer, there is also a Hex Land Tile & Dice holder and Lid. This conveniently holds all the hex Land tiles and one set of dice.

As with the Space Hex Organizer, it also has holes underneath to allow you to easily push out tiles without damaging the sides. An edge is also exposed allowing you to remove tiles that way as well.

The Hex Land Tile & Dice holder conveniently sits atop the board, with plenty of room to allow for future expansion.

We truly believe that this is the best and most compact storage solution for Tapestry, utilizing the existing storage and maximizing space.

This is your chance to get a bespoke product, a beautiful and functional storage solution for Tapestry.

This is a simple campaign. Unlike last time, we already have the 3D printer in-house and configured. In addition, we have since acquired a second T-REX 3D printer, doubling our capacity. This campaign is for the Tapestry Storage Solution. Printing and fulfillment can begin as soon as the campaign ends. We have tried to make this easy, so you can get to playing more quickly.

Skrap Landz is a modular set of 3D printable terrain compatible with Warlayer 4.0.  https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/warlayer/warlayer-3d-printable-terrain-0/description 

Once these files are printed, they can then be slid and stacked together to create large structures great for line-of-sight blocking or scaling with your miniatures.

 With your support we are offering over 50 Stl files and any stretch goals that are met. This includes but is not limited to:  

  • Concrete and Skrap walls
  • Platforms
  • Doorways

Our goal is to provide quality terrain, that is both game friendly and modular. All the models shown have been test and we will continue to test new models throughout the Kickstarter.

Story

Take up arms as a hero in ancient Japan, working together with your allies in a fully cooperative and solo game to fight back a series of three powerful demons: the Onimaru. Each round, you will roll and allocate dice to attack your enemies, use your Techniques, and manage your defenses. Break through the swarm of Hordelings to defeat all three Oni before the end of the final night...fail, and the Shadow Gate will remain open forever!

Story

 For years, inventor Stephen P. Hincks has been developing a set of confounding puzzles and he is now ready to present them to you, but solving these puzzles will be no ordinary endeavor. To activate this adventure you must first gain access to The Curious Elevator of Mr. Hincks.

  Unlike a normal elevator, the Hincks Elevator doesnâ€™t take you from the parking garage to the food court to acquire a delicious hot dog on a stick.  In fact, you could say this elevator doesn't even exist in the real world.  It's a construct that Mr. Hincks has developed and deployed to the internet so that he could challenge the world to solve his puzzles. 

 A pledge to this campaign will deliver to your door a parcel of physical materials that Mr. Hincks has deemed necessary to solve his enigma.  You'll be able to use his browser-based elevator to verify any solutions you might be able to derive.  A correct solution will activate the elevator and send you to the floor where the next puzzle can be found, while an incorrect solution won't move the elevator at all.

If you manage to outsmart all of Mr. Hincks's tricks and navigate through all the floors correctly, you'll find the end of his maze and, of course, a slice of personal glory.  Mr. Hincks keeps a list of all those who have run his puzzle gamut, and a casual mention that your name is on that list could make you the center of attention at your next formal gathering-- not that you'd stoop to such self-aggrandizement, of course.

  Mr. Hincks wants a fair game, so he will mail you everything you'll need to navigate his puzzles.  This will be a collection of documents, each of which he has deemed important for one reason or another.

These might include maps, articles, journal entries, photographs, and any other materials that Mr. Hincks has used to construct his labyrinth.  You will not need anything outside of these materials except a web browser. And your brain. And maybe some friends' brains.  

 Mr. Hincks is offering his cryptic puzzle parcel to a very select few.  This campaign only has one physical reward level, and that provides access to the entirety of the puzzle game.

Current Status

The puzzles found in the Hincks Elevator are in various states of development.  The elevator is deployed to www.HincksElevator.com, but you'll find it most uncooperative without the information in the cryptic puzzle parcel that Mr. Hincks is offering.  Given the current progress he's made on the puzzles and the interface, we believe that Mr. Hincks will be sending his cryptic parcel and powering on his elevator before the end of 2019.

Puzzle Purveyors

Mr. Hincks is completely occupied by his puzzle creation and is also slightly imaginary and thus has designated Team Bluefish as administrators of this campaign and the puzzles.  We are escape room enthusiasts who've played nearly 500 escape rooms across the United States.  In addition, we've designed and consulted on several escape rooms, coordinate the Phoenix Escape Room Enthusiast Meetup including organizing the inaugural PERE Puzzle Hunt, developed a mobile corporate team building puzzle system, and designed a few unpublished board games.

Risks and challenges

The most difficult part of developing a puzzle game like this one is setting the right level of difficulty. All puzzles need to be fair and solvable, but they shouldn't be too easy to pass. Every puzzle in this game is being thoroughly blind-tested before it will be released. It is our goal that the correct answer to each puzzle is self-validating and will make complete sense before it is even entered into the elevator interface. Having developed puzzles in lots of different contexts prior to this campaign, we believe that we have the knowledge and experience to create quality puzzles for this game. As Kickstarter backers, we've found that actual delivery (via mail) can be challenging. In order to deliver these games within the window that we've chosen, we limited the number of available pledges and restricted shipping to the US. We believe that by taking on a small set of supporters, we can effectively deliver on-time.

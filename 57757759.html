Hello! This is Jorge, founder of Pers and your host in this campaign page. First of all, thank you for considering supporting the Blobbies. Below I'll give you all the necessary info so you can decide if you want to support us ... or skip us in favor of some exploding zombie cat app.

What is this campaign about?

The purpose of this campaign is to complete the Blobbies' app for iOS and Android. 

This app is 75% complete and our goal is to ship it in May 2020. The app will have several sections including: the Blobbies' Personality Chart (where you can find out which Blobbie is most like you?) ...

... the Blobbies' Shape Challenge (where you must learn and remember shapes to then battle the evil Hexacones) ...

... and the Blobbiemorph! game (a "platformer" [think Mario Bros.] where you must blobbiemorph into different animal shapes to survive all kinds of obstacles and enemies).

Ivi (my right hand) made a tour video of the app (the Blobbiemorf! game is not yet included).

What are Blobbies®?

Blobbies are friendly colorful beings that can change shape at will.  

Blobbies were introduced to the world via their book: “Here Come The Blobbies”, a sci-fi fantasy adventure that has received several awards in USA including a “Book of the Year” award from Creative Child Magazine. 

 The full color 40-page eBook will be sent to you even if you pledge $1! 

Each Blobbie is the physical representation of one of the 7 rainbow colors and has a unique personality type based on that color. It's based on research on color theory, psychology, metaphysics, chakras, etc. (or to summarize: witchcraft!!!).

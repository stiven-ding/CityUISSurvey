Story

Spook or Smash is a fast paced, highly competitive card game. The goal is to be the first person to get rid of all their cards. The deck is made up of spooky creatures and special cards for extra spook and fun.

This game was created by Jordan Paulus, Bryan Kitchener and Zachary Barfield - three friends from work. The idea came to us in October, or Spooktober as we liked to call it. We think it combines our love of fun, spookiness and smashing the competition.

So if you're into all things spooky and enjoy fast moving and competitive games, please help us make this game a reality!

Happy Haunting!

Jordan, Bryan and Zack

There are 4 suits of cards - vampires, witches, werewolves and the undead. Gameplay follows the same pattern as SlapJack. You need at least 2 players to play but there is no limit to the amount of players. Whoever loves spooky season most goes first. Distribute the cards evenly. Then, you start by putting down cards into a middle pile and saying what turn number it is. Once you hit 13, start over. If a player puts down a number card and it matches the number said out loud, everyone needs to place their hands in the middle. Last person to put their hand in has to take up all the cards. 

In addition to the 4 suits, there are also Reverse, Spook and Hocus Pocus cards to keep things interesting. Reverse means you start placing cards going in the opposite direction. Spook means you pick a random number and start counting from there. And Hocus Pocus means immediately smash the pile. Last person in takes all the cards.

The first person to get rid of all their cards wins!

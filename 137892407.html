Story

With this special bonus content Kickstarter we are trying to finish up these three mini-adventures to expand on the events that will take place in between Folio #23 & Folio #25.    We also hope that if you are simply looking for some cool dungeon  encounters  for characters level 10+, these adventures will fill that  void.

What are in the mini-adventures? Each mini-adventure is roughly 10 encounter points in a scenario that blends in with the main Folio adventure it corresponds to (in this case, we are hoping to 'bridge'  certain scenario points within the Kasbah Dungeon from The Curse of  Roslof Keep campaign). This includes maps, and statistics in both  classic AD&D and 5th Edition D&D.    However, these can also easily work as stand alone encounters for   other ongoing campaigns.  They are designed for 4-6 characters from

What are The 5 Champions of Hate? As the corruption of unknown entities continues to press on the Kasbah Dungeon, the characters must find this aside and enter the pseudo-realm of the streambenders and destroy their 5 champions!

What can be found in Keeper of the Lost City? Pocket dimensions are the new playground of the Shard of the Infernal Machine, and within this scenario the characters will find themselves in a ruined city, being hunted by a creature of untold power.

What can be expected from Secrets of the 7th Level of Roslof Keep?  Did your characters really believe they were done with Roslof Keep?  Within this scenario they will return to where their careers all began to face a sub-level of Roslof Keep never before delved by any adventuring band! 

What are in Folio #20 -#23? These are two full adventures that include a gazetteer section and adventure section.  They are based in original AD&D format with 5th Edition D&D stat blocks, so they can be played in both systems.  They have both 2D  'Blue' hex maps as well as full 3D graphic art dungeons, and they  comprise the first 2 levels of the 6 level Kasbah Dungeon campaign.   These modules are built for 6 characters, levels 10+.

What were the 1st Bridge trio of mini-adventures? These  three mini-adventures bridged the timeline between the original Roslof  Keep dungeon and the new Kasbah Dungeon, allowing the DM to transition  old characters into the new setting while getting a bit of a preview to  what they will be expecting.

What were the 2nd Bridge trio of mini-adventures? These three mini-adventures introduce the characters to the concepts of the Amber Doors.  These pocket side dungeons will help expand the treasure and experience of the campaign while also fleshing out the greater story.

What is the Folio? The Folio is a OSR inspired adventure path that incorporates both 1E and 5E Dungeons & Dragons mechanics.    Currently, three full campaigns exist within its volumes including  the  six part Roslof Keep Campaign, the twin trilogies of The Complete   Cities of Sorcery, and the six part island adventure campaign of The   Complete White Ship.  That's 19 full Folio adventure and dozens of   mini-adventures in three huge PDF super adventure volumes.   That's a lot of gaming!

What is a 'Complete' set?  The Complete books are  compiled editions (including bonus mini-adventures and gaming  supplements) that give a DM everything they need to play the entire  campaign with ease.  The Complete Roslof Keep includes all the standard Folio content from Folio #1-#6 as well as all mini-adventures associated with it.  The Cities of Sorcery does the same for Folio #8-#13, The Complete White Ship Campaign includes Folio #14-#19, The Artifacts of Adventure includes Folio Digital Quarterly #1-#5 as well as True Level Adventures #1-#3, The Storyteller's Arcana is a grab bag of DM and player alternate content, and The Fiendish Folio creates 115 new monsters.  ALL books are in both 1E AD&D, and 5th Edition D&D mechanics!

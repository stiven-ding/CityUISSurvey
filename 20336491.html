Story

Need finely crafted metal coins for your current RPG campaign, board games or metal coins for Dwarven Smithy?  

Here's your chance to get beautiful, zinc-alloy, metal coins! You can get coins in sets of 50 or 100, and you can also add-on multiple sets of 50 coins to any pledge level to raise your collection to 100, 200 or even 500 coins. Great for any any RPG campaign or can be used with other tabletop games as coins or counters.

We'd like to say a special thanks to our artist, Karin Wittig, who designed all the coins and the special collector coin. We'll be using Quartermaster Logistics as our fulfillment partner again on this campaign. Here are our Kickstarter Goals:

  • Give backers an opportunity to purchase dwarven themed metal coins for any tabletop game or RPG adventure in different quantities
  • Give backers an opportunity to purchase metal coins for their Dwarven Smithy game
  • Give backers an opportunity to purchase metal coins plus a Dwarven Smithy game
  • Offer a special Dwarven Smithy collector coin
  • Delivery by December 2019

SPECIAL COLLECTOR COIN 

The special collector coin displays the Dwarven Smithy logo on one side and the other side can be used as a First Player token in other games. All reward levels receive this special collector coin and a black silk bag.

Risks and challenges

The molds for the coins have already been created. We expect the coins to be finished and delivered to us in the USA within 65 days after the Kickstarter ends. Our first Kickstarter was fulfilled on time and we don't expect delays or problems with this time. As a small publishing company, we relying on Kickstarter backers to supply the capital necessary to meet minimum minting and shipping costs. We will produce a quality product and it will be delivered on time. If there are any issues or problems, we will let you know and communicate with you regularly. We are backers too and want to treat you as we want to be treated when we back other projects.

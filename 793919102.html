Worm Hole to Uranus

We take a dizzy bat to the traditional drinking game.

How do you play? Good question. Here's a good answer.

First you are assigned characters based off stereotypical space movie archetypes. 

Each character has varying skill sets and hilarious quirks that you must act out.

Chart a journey from Earth to Uranus, humanity's new home after the destruction of our home planet. Along the way there are a variety of missions that the crew encounters and must be completed to avoid real consequences and ultimately, terribly boring space deaths.

Some missions use basic RPG elements and dice rolling to be completed.

 Some missions need to be completed through physical challenges.

 And some are just for fun.

There are hilariously real consequences for failing missions. And not to mention...

DEATH.

But ultimately once you get to a galaxy far far away... you'll be far far gone.

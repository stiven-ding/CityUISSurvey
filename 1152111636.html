Story

My name is Randy Butterfield, and I’ve been designing Playing Cards, an hour or two each night, for over seven years now. Some of my past designs include the ORNATE, Wild West, Rome, Draconian, Honeybee and POLLOCK Decks.

Recently, I’ve started to design a deck a year that’s geared toward my home location of Indiana. The game of Euchre is HUGE in Indiana! It also very popular in the state where I grew up - Michigan. I remember many late nights playing Euchre during my studies at Central Michigan University.

Unlike many Playing Card games, Euchre has no need for the Two through Eight Cards. So each of the custom Decks available includes two complete Euchre Decks in one box. Two for one!

HOW DO I PLAY EUCHRE?

Popular in many parts of the world, especially the American midwest, Euchre is a trick-taking card game most commonly played with four people - two teams of two. The first team to reach 10 Points wins. Please click on THIS LINK to see the rules in detail. The game may seem a little daunting at first glance, but it’s easy to learn and a blast to play!

V3 EUCHRE DECK:

The EUCHRE V3 Deck includes two full Euchre Decks inside one Tuck Box (Green and Blue).

The Back Cards feature an elaborate and ornate baroque design. Each Green and a Blue set of Face Cards includes the full suits of nines, tens, Jacks, Queens, Kings and Aces (2-8 and Jokers are not needed in most Euchre games). The semi-custom Courts incorporate the same elements from the Back Cards, in a gold colored backdrop setting. They are extremely functional and provide the highest level of playability.

Also included are two Rules Cards and four custom Score Cards. You just need a dime as your points marker. No need for boring fives as makeshift Score Cards in the EUCHRE V3 Deck!

The Tuck Box features an elegant and intricate design that’s printed in 4-Color Process.

INDIANA EUCHRE DECK

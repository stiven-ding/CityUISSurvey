PLAY CARDS, PLAY MUSIC - HAVE FUN WITH YNTUNZY

Yntunzy is a new concept in fun and learning that brings together the two greatest pastimes: card games and music! Bring the joy of “old-fashioned” games plus music to the hands of children and adults living in the digital age. Anyone can play with the cards from youngsters learning go-fish to experts playing bridge or musical matching.

Whether you love to play rummy or poker, crazy eights or bridge, music cards create a fun environment for all with a bonus – a new understanding of how to read music. Whether you already have a background in music or you are just curious about the music world, Yntunzy cards are for you. Start playing and discover a new favorite game!

Why music and cards? Music has the power to bring people together, to evoke feelings, to provide shared experiences and to inspire us all. Anyone can enjoy music. Yntunzy cards bring music to life in games and as a great learning tool for music notation and a bit of music theory. Plus, we want to help by sharing the joy of music so hundreds of school children are already playing with Yntunzy cards donated to public schools in New York and Maryland. 

The app and what's next: The music solitaire app is in beta available for Android and iOS. It is great for ear training too. 

What will we do with the funds we are raising?

  • We will donate more cards to schools.
  • We will print and offer card decks based on bass clef.

More cards – More music – More fun!


The idea for Yntunzy cards started about 20 years ago. My children loved to play games but sometimes got distracted while learning to play musical instruments. I wanted to help my children, so I became acquainted with the world of music and then got the idea for Yntunzy cards as a way to help my children learn to read music the same way they were learning numbers - by playing games.  

The design and the game mechanics incorporate concepts of music into a deck of cards that can play old games and new ones while making it fun for those who don’t know music. The basic deck introduces a limited range of musical notes and I wanted there to be more. Then I realized that the game would be better with expansion decks to include more musical concepts like sharps and flats. From there it was obvious that there could be more suits and you could play new games with six or more suits. The years of development (my children are in college) have been successful – Yntunzy is proud to be launching this music card deck concept that can best be described with the slogan More cards – More music – More fun!

Risks and challenges

When the project is fully funded, the cards have to be ordered so it will take a few weeks to deliver the individual decks. The companion app for solitaire and card reading, intended to enhance the game experience, came out in January. Funding would make it possible to provide more features for the app including a scoreboard. The card reader is in beta and would require significant investment to be perfected - a long term goal.

Risks and challenges

We have successfully kickstarted many previous projects. These Kickstarters collectively entailed sending more than a quarter million products to tens of thousands of backers. The first 1981 copies of Ghoul Island Act 1 are already printed and sitting in our warehouse. There is virtually no risk to getting these rewards into backers' hands. The second print run will commence shortly after the Kickstarter, and since it will be an identical product to the first print run, we anticipate no risk to getting it printed (at the same printers) and shipped to backers.

Greetings Adventurer!

We are SkullSplitter Dice, and we'd like your help to bring these polyhedral dice sets for RPG's to life. The three color combinations in this Kickstarter are: Blue and White (Valkyrie's Sky), Purple and White (Aether Breach), and Green and White (Forest Queen's Radiance). 

This project will be fully funded at $6,000, which unlocks Valkyrie's Sky. Additional color combinations are unlocked at $11,000 and $16,000. 

We are passionate about story telling, and wanted to tell stories around these dice sets and their names, so we decided to have three talented writers create tales if the Kickstarter funds at the $6k level. 

These three original adventures delivered electronically for all "Two Axes" ($60) and above and Early Bird (EB) backers.  Each adventure module is compatible with 5e and will fill approximately one and a half to two hours of game time.

Risks and challenges

There are timeline, manufacturing, and distribution risks with this project; we believe that we've properly accounted for them and have included time and expenses for issues with manufacturing, shipping, and distribution. All of our dice are inspected twice before being sent to our customers. We have a history of delivering on time for Kickstarters, and have been working with our manufacturing partner for many years. We believe we've accounted for variables as it relates to this project, but shipping, customs and production delays may occur. We have a 100% satisfaction 30 day guarantee for our dice.

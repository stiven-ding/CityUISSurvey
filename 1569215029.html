Story

In the SUPER RPS strategic card game, you represent one of the three elemental forces in a duel of wits, using all of your cunning to manipulate them into exposing themselves to your ruthless attack.

Slice, crush, and smother your enemies in a game of shifting fortunes and short-lived alliances.

In Super RPS, you develop your strategy by planning your hand. Each turn you'll choose whether to play cards to change your identity, change the rules of the game, hold cards to bait and switch in the combat, or attack another player with overwhelming force. 

The rules are simple, but mastering the game is not!

Each player gets three Soldier cards, playing with one at a time. If you are attacked, and cannot defend yourself, you lose your current Soldier. Lose all three of your Soldiers, and your game is over!

From your hand, Combat cards can be played to Attack another player's Soldier. Combatants take turns adding, dividing and multiplying each other's Attack and Defend scores until one wins.

Alter Reality cards establish which Soldiers can can attack and whom they can attack.

A player can play a Morphing card to temporarily transform their Soldier. Paper, Rock, or Scissors can become Scissors, Rock, or Paper.

If you don't like how things are going, you can use a Trade Hands card. Mix up everyone's plans as everyone passes their Hands around, or take exactly what you need from one player who's tilted their hand.

 A special category of cards - That's Enough of That cards - allow you to cancel various effects, sometimes on other players' turns!

Expansion content -- Advanced Rules are optional cards for your game. Drawn and played during a player's turn, but changing the fundamental rules about when and how other cards can be played, opening up new strategies!

Advanced Rules will be unlocked when the first Goal is achieved.

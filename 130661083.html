Willy is the fun loving protagonist of Nut Punch! Everywhere he goes, people want to do damage to his junk. He's not sure why his jewels are under constant threat, but after dealing with the unwarranted attacks most of his life, Willy has developed a wide array of defenses to protect his bits.

Remember how we all tuned in to America's Funniest Home Videos just to watch the guys get hit in the junk with baseball bats, rakes, and any kind of ball you can think of?! Add that to a card game, and you have Nut Punch! 

Nut Punch! is a fast-paced attack and defend card game with simple gameplay in mind. Developed by Heath Flor and Matt Rowntree (the drunken masterminds behind 1x5 Games), the game was sparked after the boys attempted to play a round of Munchkin after a long night of chugging whisky and swigging beers. They failed miserably!

No offense to Steve Jackson, we were seriously that drunk. 

The game is a blend between trick taking and War. And probably a dozen other games we haven't played. 

Attack cards are highlighted in red, while Defense is highlighted in blue. The values are inverted in the splat of the card art. Attack and Defense values are purposely clear; even if you're seeing double after a long night of partying, you and your friends will still be able to play a few rounds before passing out!

During your turn you may attack any player at the table. If they successfully defend with an equal, or higher value, you both discard your cards into the discard pile.

Make your attacks strong, and you'll blast through their defenses! With a successful attack, the attack card gets placed in the discard pile, while the defense card is flipped over in front of the player you just violated.

You must always play a single card when being attacked. If you do not have a defense card, you must play an attack card. No matter what the value is, you lose against the attacking player. Flip the card over and place it in front of you. If it is your third loss against a successful attack, you lose the game.

Riotous behavior is welcomed, and teaming up to wear down your opponents is expected! Players aren't able to draw more cards until it's their turn, so make sure to make them suffer!

First player out has to make the next round of drinks! By the time drinks are made, the game will be ready for you to rejoin! If not, you can sneak some sips of everyone's drink while they aren't looking. Don't worry, they won't notice...they're having fun playing the game, and you aren't.

Story

Lost in the realm of Gods and spirits, where dreams are the fabric of reality and souls are reborn. On the path to reincarnation, souls must shed their worldly memories. But you have refused to give up your past; a kindred spirit has ignited a flame inside of you...

Evergate is an expansive 2D puzzle-platformer featuring a unique align-to-shoot mechanic. Unleash your Soulflame and harness energy trapped within crystals to solve puzzles, find lost memories, and save your kindred spirit.

Players guide a child-like soul named Ki on her journey through the afterlife. This modern fable was inspired by movies, games, and legends we love. It draws from the wonder of The Neverending Story, the hand-drawn aesthetic of Child of Light, and the Greek myth of Orpheus' descent into the underworld.

  •  A HEARTWARMING EPIC spanning life & death and space & time 
  •  100+ PUZZLES with open-ended solutions
  •  10+ CRYSTALS with unique powers that can be creatively combined 
  •  MYTHICAL AFTERLIFE steeped in lore
  •  COLOSSAL BATTLES against the Gods and nightmares come to life
  •  HAND-DRAWN ARTWORK creates wonder in every moment 
  •  RICH SOUNDTRACK evokes the magic of Ki's journey

Legend tells that the Gods forge each soul's heart—its flame—from the ancient power of the Source. But it's the unique connection to your kindred spirit that has ignited your Soulflame and awakened the Source inside you.

Align crystals between you and the Source and fire your Soulflame to unleash their extraordinary powers. Use these powers to reach the unreachable, confront your foes, and banish the nightmares of your past back to the shadows.

There are more than 10 different types of crystals, each with a unique ability. Combine multiple crystals for even greater effect.

Evergate blends cerebral puzzles with skill-based execution. The goal of each puzzle is to reach the gate at the end however you can. Some players may opt for a straightforward route while others will strive for the more difficult complete solution using every crystal. This offers challenges for casual and hardcore gamers alike.

All of Evergate's puzzles are open-ended. These creative playgrounds encourage experimentation and embrace a spirit of trial and error. Messed up your jump? See a more fun solution? Hold the reset button to quickly try a new approach.

A second player can control Ki's spiritual guide. Friends and family can join Ki on her adventure and work through the puzzles alongside you.

The Gods have scattered your memories and taken your kindred spirit deep into the heart of the Afterlife. Search for your memories and reclaim what was lost.

The Afterlife is full of fantasies and dangers arranged into unique domains, each ruled by a deity. You'll explore four of these domains and their native crystals. As you recollect your memories, you unlock new crystals and areas to explore.

Your scattered memories have torn rifts in the fabric of time & space. The Source has brought your nightmares to life and they are bleeding into this reality. Enter the portals to your past and solve the tangled puzzles within. Traverse surreal dreamscapes and reach the gate at the end to recollect your memories.  

While the Source fuels your Soulflame, it is not yours alone. It breathes life into fantastical flora and wondrous creatures. Yet in the shadows, untold nightmares feed off its power and grow...  

Even your recollections are not safe from its ancient energy. Your childhood fears have been brought to life by the Source. To recover your memories, you must first overcome your demons.

But brandishing your Soulflame invites unwanted attention. The deities who maintain the natural order and wield celestial abilities stand in your way. On your quest to find your kindred spirit and reclaim your memories, you must confront these massive, stage-sized deities.

Curious about the gameplay mechanics of Evergate? Wondering about who exactly the developers are? Want to know why the heck we want to make this game? Well, all of those questions (and more!), are answered in our "Behind The Afterlife" video.

Stone Lantern Games is a budding indie game studio founded by 4 MIT graduates. Like creating a character in an RPG, each engineer has a specialty: Narrative, Visual Effects, Art or Music.

Kent - Lead

After finishing Elder Scrolls: Oblivion in two sleepless weeks, Kent emerged from the basement of his childhood home with a desire to create an epic story of his own. He began his own journey by learning to code and how to tell stories. While building software for robots, Kent invited his friends from MIT on the chase for that dream—of bringing an entire world to life in a video game.

Ariel - Architect

Ariel's passion for game development began at the early age of 7. His favorite games would often inspire thoughts of new abilities, units, and world lore to enrich the experience. This ongoing desire to tweak existing games evolved into a life long trend of making strange and experimental game fragments. Studying computer science and physics in college have honed Ariel's talents at building procedural animations and interactive media. Kent has concisely summarized him as "a true painter of numbers, with a vast collection of formulas ready to craft any moment."

Cynthia - Artist

Cynthia is the visual mastermind behind the beautiful world of Evergate. She also happens to be an MIT-educated electrical engineer. When she's not showcasing her art, she might be building destructive robots with her Battlebots team or cosplaying as her favorite characters from Overwatch. 

M. R. Miller - Composer

M.R. was inspired by games like Final Fantasy 7 and Sonic the Hedgehog to become both a composer and a software engineer. He likes mashups of the two disciplines, e.g. he once programmed dancing robots for an opera for the Prince of Monaco. These days, he works on his own music (Planet Diver, Slime-san) and alongside other composers on films (Power Rangers, Fate of the Furious) and games (ABZÛ, Pode).

Evergate is currently under active development. We've shown off early versions to convention-goers at PAX East & West, Boston FIG, and elsewhere. The reception has been overwhelmingly positive: Evergate was selected for PAX Rising; won the Audience Choice award at BFIG; and was nominated for The 2018 Bit Award's Game of the Year and Best Style.

Risks and challenges

This is our first game so we have and will continue to face new challenges. But at this point, we've laid a solid technical foundation and created all the building blocks we need to construct the final game. The first divine domain is done, but there are still three more to go, as well as the mind-warping finale! There's still tons of work ahead, like console ports, testing, and the full soundtrack. Not one of us questions whether the game *will* happen, only *when*. We are all thoroughly committed to the project and have been for three years already. Our biggest shortfall right now is time. A successful Kickstarter will afford us more time to work on the game as well as additional personnel. With the proper resources, we are aiming to launch Evergate in November 2019. As we progress towards launch, we plan to keep the Kickstarter community appraised of our progress with frequent, regular status updates. Keep us honest and follow along as we code the game, create its artwork, and compose its music. Making a game is hard, but we're a driven, passionate team. Our time at MIT and as engineers in the real world have given us the skills needed to make Evergate a reality. And with your help, together we can bring this dream to life.

  IMPORTANT NOTE:  Kickstarter does not allow funds to be used as a donation to charity.  100% of Kickstarter funds will be used to launch Trumps & Ladders into the world (creating, manufacturing, marketing, etc.).  Mythica Gaming pledges to donate a percentage of our profits from sales of the game to Amnesty International. 

Risks and challenges

The biggest risk we think we face is a challenge from the current U.S. presidential administration who may not approve of the game. We intend to handle any objections in a gracious but firm stance on the 1st amendment of the constitution on the freedom of speech. The 2nd challenge we hope to face is an avalanche of backers. Given the current political climate, we recognize the game may strike a nerve. We have successfully launched 3 games, and have a working relationship with 2 major game manufacturers. We will leverage those relationships to produce and distribute the product to our backers as quickly and efficiently as possible.

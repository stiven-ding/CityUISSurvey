Shine: A Wind Turbine That Fits in Your Backpack




Story

HOW DO YOU POWER YOUR OUTDOOR ADVENTURES? Love hiking and camping, but worry about not having power if there's an emergency? Wind power is one of the largest producers of clean energy in the world, yet most people don't have direct access to it. It's time to take wind power into your own hands and now you can with Shine. The Shine Turbine is the ultra-compact, highly efficient, portable wind charger that lets you use the wind to rapidly create and stores clean energy to power handheld devices like your camera and drone day or night wherever the wind blows. From exploring coasts to navigating plains to scaling mountains, all you need to do is set up Shine and in under 2 minutes, you’ll have a wind generator to power your adventures. You can charge it whenever you want and its battery can fully power things like your iPad or phone. 12.000mAh battery can charge up to 4 devices and 3 phones worth of power can be collected in as little as an hour. Access to renewable power off-grid is essential as electronics often require more charging power than portable batteries can provide. Solar panels don't always work when you need them to. Generators are loud and expensive and battery packs always die. Portable power stations are too heavy and it's easy to forget to charge battery packs.

With Shine you have the freedom to charge and explore on your own terms, day and night, rain, cloud, or shine. Don't know where the wind's coming from? No problem! Shine automatically turns into the wind as long as the leaves are rustling, you’re in business. An umbrella may not be able to handle high winds But Shine can. As nature lovers and outdoor adventurers, we spent the last three years developing a product, delivers all the benefits of a large wind turbine, scaled down to fit in your backpack.

It's super lightweight with the highest power-to-weight ratio compared to solar panels water turbines and thermoelectric stoves. This means you're getting the most power for the least amount of weight you have to carry. Also, it's incredibly quiet with maximum of 50 dB! Shine has distinctive advantages
(1) The turbine weighs 3 lbs and collapses into the size of a water bottle so you can have access to wind power wherever you go.
(2) Set up takes 2 minutes or less with everything you need stored inside the turbine.
(3) Access energy whenever you need it or precharge Shine via a wall outlet.
(4) Power your devices even when it's raining and withstand sand, dirt, and dust build-up.
(5) Doesn’t require a phone, Bluetooth, or any other device to generate power.
(6) Produce clean wind energy and reduce your ecological impact.
(7) Durability: made of aircraft-grade aluminum, and injection-molded polycarbonate-reinforced plastic to withstand the wear and tear of the outdoors. With Shine you have renewable lightweight energy to power things like your camera and drone. Use Shine’s internal battery on-the-go to charge any handheld USB device, including phones, tablets, lights, drones, cameras, and more, or charge from the turbine while its operating. You can even pre-charge before you take off! Shine is for anyone who wants to charge handheld devices while disconnected from the energy grid. The turbine is perfect for keeping electronics charged while backpacking, camping, RVing, fishing, hunting, remote working, and more. You will have a non-stop source of power to keep your phone running and a light to see in the dark. Head out on an adventure!

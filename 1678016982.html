Welcome! Dark Room is a single-player story-based immersive film development simulation with mystery horror game elements.

Here are just some of the mysteries you might encounter. 

  • A former film developer has left a haunting message - Solve their disappearance by digging through the old files and film canisters they left behind.
  • The photo orders reveal disquieting motives and relationships between the townsfolk. Uncover the terrible truth behind the city.
  • Assist the police with strict confidentiality on a string of murders.
  • A stalker is leaving mysterious packages at the store. They are too crazy to be believed, but they cannot be ignored.
  • Hidden messages spell the convening of a secret unseen cult.
  • Is there a strange creature stalking in the mists.
  • What other horrors lay ahead? - Find out in The Dark Room

Story

Coming off of my first Kickstarter making dice sleeves, I wanted to continue my love of woodworking and table top gaming.    I want to take the things I've learned and continue to create high quality wooden tabletop gaming hardware. 

These spell trackers will be milled and carved out of solid pieces of beautiful hardwood.  Each will be accompanied by a set of 9 12mm Chessex D6s so you can start using them immediately.  I will start by offering the spell trackers in six different woods across the main three tiers and four different dice choices for the dice.  I'll get backer feedback on dice selection.

Spell Trackers are 2" tall, 5.5" wide, and .75" thick.  Slots are custom cut to hold a 12mm die and the large vault holds all 9 die.

You can use the tracker in two different ways.  You can start with your slots empty and add die as you cast and rotate up to your max.  You can also start with all your slots at your max number and then count down as you use spells, moving the die to the large vault if you use all the spells for that level.

Each spell tracker will be made by me in my small workshop in Virginia.

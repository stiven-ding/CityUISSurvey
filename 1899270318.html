The idea for Extinction was floating around in our minds about a year ago.  We were always huge dinosaur fans obsessed with card games, but we couldn't find a dinosaur card game that could satisfy us.  That's when we thought, "Let's make our own card game"!  We made the initial prototype over many all-nighters.  Grabbed some images online, cut them out, glued them on index cards, and laminated them.  Over the next several months, we play tested with hundreds of people, worked out mechanics and card ratios.  Until, we finally had something that kept us up all night playing the game until 2am on a work night.  We're so excited to show you all this card game.  We know you'll love it as much as we do!

Risks and challenges

We have already found our manufacturer and have shipping set up. As of right now, the only challenge is if we get overwhelming demand (good problems), we might need to find more manufacturers to keep up supply.

Story

Virtual Build A School, is all about raising money to build schools.  Every year online games raise Billions of dollars. It occurred to me if we could redirect or tap into a small portion of that we could build a lot of schools.  My game does just that. We ask players to donate money to buy the purchase of a new school building. For example a man in Cleveland donates 100 dollars which could go towards the purchase of bricks needed to build the school.  Now his avatar will lay the bricks in place.  Once all the work in cyberspace is complete the construction in reality begins. 

These funds will be held in each participating schools educational account to further ensure safety.  

Risks and challenges

The only risk or challenges facing this project is the possibility of not meeting the projected pledge amount needed to build the school.

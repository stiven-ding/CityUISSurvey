Story

Castra is a game where strategic decision making can turn the tide from victory to defeat. 

In Castra, you will direct an entire army’s movements and attacks. As you begin conquering territory after territory, you must think like a commander and balance available resources and supplies throughout your army. The design of each military camp affects production levels, which should exceed the needs of your soldiers. Without housing, food, medical care, and more, your troops won’t survive on the battlefield. 

Build: Each territory is controlled from a Castrum (military camp) that is occupied by soldiers. In order to keep the army healthy and fed, different buildings such as barracks, watch towers, and medical tents will have to be constructed. Balancing resources and preparing enough defenses can make the difference between victory and defeat when an attack comes.

Fight: Varying unit combinations require strategic decision before going into battle. Every unit has an advantage against some unit types and weaknesses against others. Which means unit combinations will play a critical role in the outcome of battles. This rock-paper-scissors style of balance creates strategic depth in the game.

Conquer: The world map is used to plan attacks, and which territories need reinforcement. Controlling more territory will gain the player more resources and soldiers. But spreading troops out too thin while conquering territory will create vulnerabilities for the enemy to attack.

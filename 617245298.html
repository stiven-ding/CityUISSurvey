Cards for Math!

Explore a brave new world of knowledge

Our educational system has been giving a hard time to kids when it comes in learning Mathematics. Teachers trying to create challenging tutoring sessions for students constantly fail in getting them interested in math, using the old traditional way.

Math cards are a promising project

We are dedicated in providing students with a great way to learn math and be introduced in the magic of numbers. In this project we have the ambition to create high-quality cards that depict all the numbers as well as the signs of the basic arithmetic exercises and the equations.

After years of teaching math in a vast variety of schools, it has been shown that students are always bored of this lesson. The electronic calculators are putting barriers to the educational procedure since students cannot feel the touch of real numbers.

This project has been carefully planned to create a series of cards that will be given to you so that teaching math can become an easy and entertaining task. Given that children like to play cards, their vivid appearance will get them interested in knowing and touching each number combined with equation signs, square roots and arithmetic exercises signs.

We are ambitious enough to believe that this method will securely introduce young students to the basic principles of math and make them consider it like a game. Years of in class experience would be in your hands to use it while teaching math to your own children or students.

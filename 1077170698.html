Risks and challenges

Game development can be a harsh taskmaster, with time and financial constraints often providing unexpected challenges that can ultimately lead to changes and delays. Fortunately, We are determined and motivated to see this project through no matter what obstacles we may face. Another challenge is to find the perfect balance between our own ideas for Grandma and the expectations of the community. We’re well aware that many horror fans are passionately attached to the genre and understand and adore its intricacies deeply. That’s why over the coming months – and during this very Kickstarter campaign – we’ll be consulting the community to help create an experience that will be beloved by all.

Story

Unicorn Power Battle is a card game all about Unicorns, Dragons and Gems! 

You begin the game as a pony, battle dragons with gems, collect Unicorn Magic and earn all three Elemental Items required to become a Unicorn, Pegasus and eventually a Magestic Alicorn (that’s a unicorn with wings)!

Designed by a 7 year old, it’s easy enough for YOUNG kids to learn and adults enjoy the silly cards, battles and strategy!

For every game we sell, we will donate a game to Toys for Tots, so less fortunate kids can also enjoy a magical holiday!






Risks and challenges

We have worked hard to ensure there is very little risk to the project: + The game has been fully designed and it has been tested by both adults and children + We have selected a factory and they have provided us samples of the completed game (you can see the samples in the video) + Fulfillment plans have been lined up for speedy distribution

Risks and challenges

Our risks and challenges are the same as any other starting board game. In order to keep our price per board game affordable, we need to order a minimum amount of units from our manufacturer. If we do not hit our minimum funding goal then there is no way for us to fund the first printing of this game at a reasonable price. This total was based on our most recent quote from a manufacturing company on the VIXIV game package as it stands. It includes other costs such as space, transport, some advertising costs, and other expenses related to the marketing and sale of VIXIV. Our personal money, credit, and sanity is invested in this project. Through backing VIXIV, you are helping us achieve a goal that we are passionate and have worked extremely hard on for the past 3 years.

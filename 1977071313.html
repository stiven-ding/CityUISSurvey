Story

What we do - Cigar Box Battle Mats are high quality, fleece terrain mats created by gamers for gamers. They fold up, don't wrinkle, travel light (i.e. just throw them in your backpack), are washable, and allow you to set up great looking games in minutes!  

Cigar Box Battle Mats are professionally illustrated and will make your gaming table come alive! They are specifically designed to allow gamers to set up great looking games quickly for club night, at conventions or at home with friends. 

 Do you like rolling hills? Just place anything underneath the mats to create scenic, rolling hills (we use styrofoam, towels, and/or small bean bags), place the mat on your game table, and layer with buildings and terrain! It's just that easy! You will be rolling dice in minutes!  

We offer mats with just a base color design that you can layer with your own roads, woods and streams, and we offer the other extreme with mats that have roads, woods, and stream sprinted on them for even faster set up! The choice is yours! 

Size - The mats measure over 6 feet x 4 feet  (approx. 77" x 59"), which gives you extra room on a standard 6'x4' table to compensate for the slight elevation changes of the hills and for some overhang. That's why we call them "6'x4' plus".

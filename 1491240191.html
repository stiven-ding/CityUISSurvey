Risks and challenges

While Gnarly Tree Games is a new company formed for the initial purpose of bringing Avast and other new games to tabletop board gamers around the world, we are no strangers to the gaming industry. While there is always risk starting a new company, we think the risks are minimal given our extensive business experience both in and outside the tabletop industry. We have partnered with Gamerati, who have proven track records planning, executing, and delivering Kickstarters. We believe their extensive, hands-on expertise, plus our other industry relationships, further mitigate these risks. In recent months international factors, such as Brexit in the UK and a growing trade war with China, introduced volatility into the publishing and tabletop games industries. Should international war break out, or a giant meteorite smash into the planet, or a giant leviathan from deep within the earth emerge, it may delay the game slightly.

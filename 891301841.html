Story

Starglades is a 2.5D platformer with RPG elements and companion combat being developed for Windows PC. The player must learn new abilities to defend innocent domains from deletion, traveling through dark and whimsical landscapes, solving puzzles, and overcoming hostile obstacles to reach this goal. The aesthetic and story of Starglades is influenced by early generations of webcomics, flash and Gameboy games, RPG concepts, and is planned to be suitable for all ages.

 Currently the Starglades demo is in the pre-production phase; all gameplay, audio, and visual effects are works in progress and subject to change, try the demo with the link below!

Starglades Pre-Production Demo Link

 A homely hub for a variety of characters and formats, Starglades is a tranquil waystation; until one night, strange events and errors begin to occur from neighboring networks. Unaware of the cause of danger, the most able characters must find the perilous source and put a stop to a mysterious menace.

Play as Basil the Copycat, a sprightly feline with the power to summon and shapeshift, Basil is put to good use by a benevolent fairy, bribing Basil with delicious food and compliments for good deeds. Traveling on land, sea and air, the pair will encounter friends and foes with their own special abilities and interest in power. 




 Starglades comes with traditional platforming features like sliding, double-jumping, and power techniques with a blend of RPG flavor! Companions and abilities are gained throughout the game, using the  help from a diverse party of characters will grant bonuses based on their domain and personality. These powerful allies and skills will add more choices to your play style, whether you are an offensive-minded stalwart or an invaluable healer supporting your friends, part of our aim is making any role viable for completion.

Throughout your journey, you gain skills from story progression, merchants, and discovering secrets. These skills can be equipped to your primary and secondary ability slots and are balanced with a mana system; taking particular forms or spells can restrict mobility based on their potential effects and limitations. Pickups in Starglades include health, mana, temporary power ups, and currency dropped by chests, enemies, level rewards, hidden areas, and NPCs.

 Levels take place in different domains of cyberspace, meaning that aesthetics and rules may be altered by the domain's nature. Domains offer the chance to gain allies or leave you empty-handed; exploration includes an adjustable camera for 2D, 2.5D, and cinematic viewing angles! 

 After the prologue, domains can be approached non-linearly for clues to your final foe. New domains will have nature, urban, and abstract cyberspace sublevels with new abilities, obstacles and character to meet.  Some events we are striving for is more enemies, obstacles, deeper progression system, platforming colossal-scale characters, and  decision-affecting interactions.  


 About the Developer

Our most recent work experience is in  conceptualizing, programming, and producing digital media for computer  games. Our latest creation is Wormhole City, a free-to-play, singleplayer, cyberpunk  adventure game that launched on Steam in December 2018. Wormhole  City involved four years of learning Epic  Games' Unreal Engine 4, using Steam integration, heavy blueprint  scripting, and small usage of C++ programming. Starting as a learning  experience, Wormhole City has been iterated and patched using player  feedback for new content in 2019, recently undergoing an engine update and overhaul.

We've been  lucky to collaborate with artists contracted by companies like Valve,  Disney, Square Enix, and Dreamworks through our work and we thrive on  inspiring and sparking imaginations in personal and online communities. The dream goal is to subside on making free, fun, interactive, and educational games, founding Zenrok Studios in 2015 with this purpose in  mind. Collaborations and opportunities with independent and industry professionals include  composers Brandon Maahs and Daniel Sandowski, voice actors Rachael Messer and Jonathon Cooke, and artists Ashely Culbertson and Xavier Differ.

 Starglades is a part-time passion project and your feedback will determine the magnitude of its development. If possible, the plan is to add more  more powers, enemies, character synergies, obstacles, interactions, levels, story arcs and polish. We value constructive criticism from experienced curators and believe the Kickstarter community offers the best advice available. All proceeds go towards developing Starglades, we have a strong foundation with our biggest priorities in creating a cohesive gameplay experience with the widest range of compatibility and optimization possible. Costs include hosting for digital platforms and any commission fees for additional gameplay, technical and artistic support. Ultimately Starglades will release for Winter 2020, with your help, we can make this a stronger everlasting memory for players to enjoy.

Risks and challenges

Having launched games on digital platforms like Steam and Itch.io previously, the biggest challenges for development will be optimizing and delivering a satisfying gameplay experience compatible to a wider range of operating systems and hardware. Using the invaluable lessons learned from Wormhole City, our ability to progress and iterate on development is faster and more focused than ever.

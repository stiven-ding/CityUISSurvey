Story


Vengeance is a revenge movie turned board game. You play a hero that has been beaten and bruised by gangs, out to exact revenge. You win by developing your hero, tracking down those who wronged you and bursting into their dens to lay your furious vengeance in action-packed fight sequences. Use your abilities to manipulate your dice into kick-arse combos to wipe out your nemesis, and possibly, their whole crew. Vengeance is a competitive game for 1 to 4 players containing over 81 beautifully sculpted miniatures.

KICKSTARTER EXCLUSIVE

We have decided to henceforth make Vengeance a Kickstarter only product. A limited number of base games, expansions and add-ons will also be available on the Pledge Manager after the campaign ends, our web-store and at game conventions Mighty Boards and Greenbrier Games attend. Retailers that buy from the campaign and Pledge Manager will also be able to sell the game.

The Vengeance base box and the Rosari expansion was translated into French, Spanish and German sold into distribution in Europe and North America. The aim here was to spread the game as much as possible to give it a longer life and a wider community. The profit on these were marginal due to production costs. In some regions we even made a loss. Due to this and a variety of other issues we decided to make Vengeance a Kickstarter only offering.

OUR TEAM

We’re a small team of experienced designers, artists and writers, based in Malta and Poland, with years of experience working on board and video games. Several members in the team are senior academics working in games research, visual arts and psychology. A few of us have also worked in the video game industry on titles like Thronebreaker:Witcher Tales and Cyberpunk 2077, among others.

The experience and knowledge we have gathered in the video game industry and game research informs a lot of the work we do at Mighty Boards. We have established a solid testing process that we deploy with all our games, coordinating test teams spread around the world. We put an emphasis on rigorous development striving for innovative mechanics that stimulate the imagination. While our games span a variety of thematic and mechanical genres, they have one thing in common: the tight coupling of theme and mechanics. Our academic and video game backgrounds have also allowed us to dream up specific experiences that we then sculpt into existence in a reflective manner.

Risks and challenges

Vengeance has already been developed in full and tested by a pool of over 80 testers around the world before the first Vengeance campaign. There have been many rounds of blind testing after the end of the campaign to ensure clarity of communication in the rulebook and the game's graphic design. Aside from this, since the game has been out for over a year there are a number of reviews that attest to the game's thematic appeal and mechanical solidity. Everything apart from the Director's Cut expansion has been already manufactured and what you see in this page is the final product. There is a limited stock reflected in the limited amounts of certain pledge levels. For the Director's Cut we will be preparing the final artwork for submission to the manufacturers after the end fo the campaign. Most of this is done, except for a few of the stretch goals we have prepared for the campaign. The whole team is very experienced in this process already so there is minimal risk in this part of the project completion. The next step is manufacturing. Due to some hiccups we had in the first printing of Vengeance we have decided to move the production and assembly of this project to the same manufacturer we worked with on Posthuman Saga, as they gave us great results and are one of the top names in the industry. Having said all of this, there may be some unforeseen circumstances beyond our control which may affect our meticulous plans. In that case we can assure you that we will do everything in our capacity to get the process back on track.

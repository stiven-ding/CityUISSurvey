Story


RLCubeshop was created with the intention to focus on our customers need. We hand magnetized Rubick’s Cube to customer desire and expectations. We are located in the beautiful Reading Pennsylvania. As a Rubicks cube solver i noticed that company often build great non-magnetized cube so i use to buy those cube and magnetized for my self one day i magnetized a couple cubes and posted them on Ebay, with an awesome success they were sold in less than two weeks, then a light bulb light up, i started to magnetized Rubik’c cube and sale them. Our purpose is to take a product well build with great material and create new boundaries with it, and make it even better with magnets. Our Cubes are carefully hand made magnetized using super strong Neodymium Disc Magnet – NiCuNiPlated.

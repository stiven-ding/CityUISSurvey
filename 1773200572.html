Story

Stray Cats is a family-friendly game. It is quick and easy to learn and takes around 10-15 mins to complete a game. It is perfect for children and family gatherings, or those who never play any board game and wanted to try out a quick game. The purpose of the game is to bring cats to a loving home. Now it has a new theme: Stray Dogs - where you can enjoy the game with dogs. 

And 2 expansion packs; you would need to have the base game (Stray Cats or Stray Dogs) to play it.

In "Housing Pets" you can sabotage your neighbors' pets collection and make an enemy out of your friends with action cards. 

In "Holiday Surprise"  and get to collect lots of nice Christmas gifts to gain more actions during your turn, but stay away from the naughty cards :D

Risks and challenges

As an individual with a passion for creating board games, and working alone requires a lot of time and effort toward the project. It is a challenge to introduce the game into the world and reach lots of potential backers since it’s a risk not to have enough support. This project requires printing four different decks Stray Cats, Stray Dogs, Housing a Pet, and Holiday Surprise. It is necessary to reach a specific fund for each deck because printing lots of copies help lower the cost of one.

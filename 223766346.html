INFRARED is an open world action-adventure where players will have the tools to create their own adventure while co-existing with civilians, crime organizations, neural-tech wildlife, and law enforcement.   

A player can choose to engage in legal activities for a modest income and never worry about encounters with the law. Illegal activities have a higher risk level yet provides the fastest source of income; however if proper precautions aren't taken, law enforcement and other players in the server will be alerted. Players who want to be apart of law enforcement must graduate from the training academy in order to become an officer. Active duty officers will be limited to 30% of the server population to maintain balance within the world. 

Ultimately players will have control over how the world functions and behaves. Players will need to be cautious of how they interact with others and the environment due to our inventory system structure. If a player is killed outside of a Safe Zone, the contents of the players inventory will be up for grabs. We encourage players invest in a home, car, and bank account to store valuable items.

We know the scope of this project is massive and hasn't been fully executed by any other studio, but we're approaching this idea realistically. We are first focusing on the core elements of the game, then we will attack each game-play feature and perfect it until we're ready to move forward. Throughout the game's evolution, we will consistently perform updates based on community feedback to shape INFRARED into the best version possible.

You can be apart of helping this game reach its fullest potential by backing our project early. This support will be the initial step into solidifying our foundation as a gaming company. We are very aware of the time, resources, energy, and dedication it will cost to produce this game and will counter that by adding more talented team members to divide and complete tasks within a timely manner so you can experience the finalized game. 

Environment - Our world is inspired by the neon lights of Shibuya, Tokyo, the grittiness of New York streets, and the warmth of the California sun. Our world has have a mind of its own—for example, a random lethal bar fight or bank heist could lead to your demise. Our world's  unique charm will leave you curious to explore. Our lead environment artist is handcrafting the world to be immersive and exciting for players. 

Power Hierarchy - We believe that everyone should have a fair chance at being the top dog in the server at some point, so we took measures to ensure that. What we call the Blood Moon Cleanse you may know as a server wipe, inspired by the movie The Purge. On the last day of the server wipe everything will be legal—players and AI's will be hunting for blood. During the Blood Moon Cleanse legal activity includes but is not limited to murder, robbery, etc. The top three players with the highest kill-streaks will receive gifts and other advantages going into the next play cycle. This structure will allow anyone to join the game and not be overshadowed by other players' who've been playing for years. 

Advancement System - Within this system contains character progression that will make the world easier for some and harder for others. In summary, INFRARED will feature attributes, skills, augmentations, and other features that can be upgraded/ranked-up by offline and online training system, to give players an edge. These perks will give you options on how to approach a scenario to reach a desired outcome. 

Legal Activities

  • Neural-Tech Animal Hunting (Hunt animals that have escaped from Neural-Tech's labs and scrap them for pay)
  • Mining (Mine rare resources to sell to techs)
  • Business Owning (Purchase businesses around the world and maintain them for a profit)
  • Fight Club (Fight AI or online competitors)
  • Zoomer Driving (Basic taxi service that players can give rides to AI or online players)
  • Waypoint Racing (Impromptu race with online players to compete for cash or pink slips)
  • Gambling (Visit the casino or local bar to play poker, Blackjack, slot machines, or pool)
  • And more!

Illegal Activities

  • Heists (Start a heist within the server inside banks, jewelry stores, gas stations, and more.)
  • Raids (Raid AI and online players' homes, warehouses, and other places for weapons, cash and other resources)
  • Data Center (Hack server rooms to sell sensitive information)
  • Drug Dealing (From small scale street dealing to large scale manufacturing)
  • Salvage Shop (Sell stolen vehicles for cash or parts)
  • And more!

Law Enforcement - Join law enforcement to restore order inside of New Phoenix City. Online officers will be joined along side by AI officers at certain times such as heist responses, bar fights, home invasions, and other major events happening within the server. Online officers can respond to AI-based calls to apprehend suspects or diffuse situations. Online officers that bend the law and go "rogue" will be fired if they're caught breaking the law while on duty. 

Property Owning & Security - Players can own multiple homes and vehicles to secure loot and change load-outs. At home, players can store weapons, ammunition, cash, tech, drugs, and other items to prevent losing them while out in the world. While a players' home is a relatively safe place, online players can manage to raid inside if the home isn't properly secured. The second safest place to store items is the player's vehicle, each vehicle has storage in the trunk and a 4 digit locking system to prevent theft. The local bank is available for players to store legal cash, if the bank if robbed it has no affect on the player's stored money. 

Other Features - 

  • Tech Station (Purchase consumables, weapons, or repair your vehicle)
  • Black Market (Clean illegal money, purchase weapons, hacking tech, and more)
  • Shopping (Purchase or change into new clothing to increase skills and attributes)
  • Tattoos (Purchase new ink)
  • Jewelry Store (Purchase precious metals and stones to increase skills and attributes)
  • Bars (Drink and party with AI and other players)
  • Pets (Purchase pets for companionship and protection)
  • Gym (Hit the gym to increase skills and attributes)
  • Bowling (Play with up to 3 other online or AI players)
  • Pool (Play with up to 1 other online or AI player)

Risks and challenges

INFRARED is our passion project, this concept was birthed four years ago after being unsatisfied with the gaming scene. The gaming industry has turned away from trying to impress the community and push the line, and has turned into a money hungry industry. We aim to put the power back in the players hands and listen to our community to fine tune the game into an experience we're all proud to be apart of. We need your support to bring this game to a level that can compete with AAA titles, and solidify our foundation as independent developers. If you have any questions or concerns be sure to join our Discord community and ask away, all of our developers, freelancers, and moderators are active and willing to help answer any questions you may have.

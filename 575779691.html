                                           Game Overview

    Champions Arena will be a 3rd person arena combat game. It will have the traditional roles of dps, healer, and tank. There will be many classes to choose from. It will focus on skill based combat and team play. There will be multiple game modes to choose from. There will be a leveling system in place. As you level you will unlock more spells/Ability's to make a build that suits your play style. There will be a reward system for winning matches that gives you gear drops. There will be ranked play with a well thought out ladder ranking to insure fun and fair play for everyone. I want the game to be competitive but also fun for casual players that just wanna jump in very now and then and have fun.i plan on having a guild system as well.

     There are plenty more features the will be shared as development progresses. More photos/videos will be added as development progresses as well. I am committed to making something thing that the gaming community will love. There will be no pay to win features at all in this game! I will be dedicated to developing this game 24/7 and will aim to get it out as fast as i can without rushing it and releasing an unfinished product. Thank you for your support! If you have any further questions or even an idea you would like me to consider adding to the game please feel free to contact me anytime!

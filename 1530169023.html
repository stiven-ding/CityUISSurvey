Cool... but do they PRINT?

These models will definitely be easier to print with a resin printer. However, I still gave thought for FDM printers. (Small elements were made as thick as possible, ridiculous overhangs were minimized, minor detail were exaggerated, etc.)

That being said, it won't be a cake walk with a FDM printer! This is partly why an all-in pledge is only $10. That way you'll be getting your money's worth even if some models are harder to print than others.

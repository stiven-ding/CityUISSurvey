  • We're also offering an ~80-page Players Guide, which is perfect for players to build and level their characters, along with providing the rules for reference.

Era: Lost Legend is a fun, faced-paced, story-driven RPG which places you in a world where technology and magic have an uneasy balance. It is inspired by the Final Fantasy games and, as a result, features complex and interesting characters, epic quests, sinister villains and world-ending threats.

Playing is accessible and enjoyable for new players and veterans alike, thanks to the Era d10 Rule Set. We have been creating games in this system for eight years and have played them with every type of player at conventions across the UK, Europe and North America.

An evil and powerful corporation is sacrificing life on the planet in an effort to control every town and city with their vast army of robots. 

However, a small rebellion, known as THORN, has vowed to put an end to this scheme!

You take on the role of individuals in this universe, able to wield weapons or magic, trying to topple the evil Stardust, Inc. Forced to fight superior numbers, you'll have to harness all of your powers to defeat them in your own epic story of friendship, heroism and the battle between good and evil!

The Era d10 ruleset is a lightweight and dynamic system which is designed to help the players experience everything from suspense-filled moments to epic combat to computer hacking with equal ease. It's also designed to provide benefits to those who roleplay their characters well, promoting the core skills that are transferable between every roleplaying game.

It's designed to allow you to experience this universe in a way that is as smooth as possible without being unrepresentative - the focus is on you and your choices, but they have every possibility of failure, especially if you're not skilled in what you attempt.

By choosing your skills carefully, your character can focus their powers into their superhuman abilities, dominate in any of five forms of combat, talk their way out of any situation or protect their teammates from harm.

In Era: Lost Legend, you have the choice to play either a magic-wielding Ethereal or a weapon-wielding Corporeal, and this adds a new dimension to the game, as Ethereals must have a Corporeal to manifest! This means that two players can choose to end up sharing one physical body, and must work together if they want to succeed. Brand new mechanics for the Era d10 Rule Set force even unwilling characters to find a compromise, or they risk mutating into a monster!

Players roll a number of d10s, attempting to roll higher than a given Success Threshold. The more dice they roll above the Threshold, the more Successes they achieve.

The Threshold rises as the action becomes more difficult to accomplish and the number of dice increases as a player becomes more skilled. While one Success might achieve a positive result, the more Successes achieved, the better the outcome is for the player.

Of course, things can also go wrong - if you roll more 1s than Successes, you will Fumble. This leads to varying (though usually hilarious!) results. And, of course, the harder an action is, the more likely it is you will Fumble!

The number of dice is defined using an Attribute and Skill system, which each define their own areas of influence, you roll your dice depending on what you're attempting. whether Dexterity + Engineering for a precision piece of work, Intelligence + Engineering for a more theoretical problem or Luck + Engineering for a complete long shot, you'll be able to adapt to your circumstances and focus on your strengths.

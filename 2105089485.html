The Goal

The purpose of this Kickstarter is funding Mech Merc Company development from its current proof of concept stage into early access launching on Steam by March 2nd 2020. If this campaign is successful, then Steam keys are to be distributed for the closed pre-alpha. The game will need a basic functioning career mode to be ready for early access, but will still need several years of development in early access to be a complete polished game.

The Game

Mech Merc Company will be an affordable community driven single player mech sim for fans of the 90s mech sims. Players will be the owner of their own mercenary company, which they will manage.

You will be able to: play missions, customize mechs, negotiate contracts, buy and sell mechs/equipment, hire/fire AI pilots, repair and rearm between missions.

The game is to be a sandbox with randomly generated contracts, with randomly generated missions, with randomly generated enemies. Considerations for story based missions will happen during early access.

 DRM Free

The game will not include any form of DRM. While store APIs like Steamworks API will be integrated into the game; they will never be used as DRM.

Mission Types

Battle - Destroy a group of enemy units.

Search and Destroy - Destroy enemy units spread out over a collection of nav points.

Assassination - Destroy a specific enemy unit and return to landing zone.

Battle Support - Destroy a group of enemy units and protect a group of friendly units.

Destroy Base - Destroy target buildings.

Defend Base - Destroy all attacking enemies and protect target buildings.

Capture Base - Destroy all base defends and do not destroy target buildings.

Destroy Convoy - Destroy target vehicles.

Escort Convoy - Protect target vehicles until they reach navigation point.

Capture Convoy - Destroy convoy escorts and do not destroy target vehicles.

Modding Support

The intention is to have steam workshop support working by the early access launch. Most data of the game will be easily edited with text editors. Modding tools are to be built during early access for more complex content like maps/mechs/weapons effects and others.

Public Domain Open Source Lore

The plan is to build lore with the community, and as result it makes sense that the community should own the lore. This means that any story elements that are part of Mech Merc Company will be freely used by anyone for anything.

Risks and challenges

The MMC team doesn't know if it is possible to get everything done in the 6 month time frame that has been allotted. Whatever the state of the game is in at 6 months it will be going into early access. The game has an active public discord: https://discord.gg/WX2BgP9 where information about the project's progress will be posted, and people can ask questions. During the closed pre-alpha on Steam, the intention is to update constantly, getting feedback, and fixing bugs along the way. Ideally with constant community feedback and assistance the game will become a fun little mech sim.

Azura's Adventure to the Mayan Lands!




Story

Hi Everyone!

 

My name is Azura and I was invited by Tz'utujil Mayan Elder ~ Tata Pedro Cruz to visit Guatemala, Lake Atitlan, to celebrate the Mayan New Year, learn about the Mayan Culture and visit ancient Mayan Temples! 

 

I will have the opportunity to celebrate Wayeb - last month of the Haab calendar Feb 16-20 and greet Mayan New Year on Feb 21. Wayeb month is special because it lasts for only 5 days and is considered a transition period, for the traditional Mayan world! The celebration is filled with art, music, traditional food, Mayan prayers, fire ceremonies and visits to sacred mayan powers spots by the Lake Atitlan.  Also visits to Mayan sacred temples like Iximche, Zaculeu, Taluk, Qunata Ixe.

 

I'm so excited to learn about the Mayan history and culture so I can carry the precious knowledge passed on to me and share it with my peers. 

 

This will also be my first time out of the country and most importantly...

What is About Wings?

I’m Robin, a tea addict, travelling through Europe in my van, Hermes. Well, recently my boyfriend has joined in in Spain.

We document our journey with the video series “About Wings” and aim to inspire others to go on their own adventures.

And so far, we have been successful: Besides the amazing emails from fans, we also got attention from well known Youtubers and even BBC World.


Why do we need Your Help?

But now we ran into a new challenge:

Hermes doesn’t have proper ventilation, which leads to mould. Also, he is simply too small for TWO people and you can’t cook inside. I'm afraid it is soon time to give Hermes some rest from all the travelling and get a new, bigger van.

Working hard, I now have more money on my account than ever before. But we are still far from reaching what we need for our new van-home... And proper vans are not cheap in Europe.

I started this crowdfunding campaign in hope for your wish for About Wings not to end, just as it has started.


What's in for You?

I have produced the series for - let’s be fair, pretty much for free - and I want to keep it this way. After all, I can see how much it benefits my audience. Season ONE is completely filmed and I am currently releasing Episodes on a weekly basis.

If you would enjoy to see Season TWO, we will need a new van.


And You can Help!

Whether it is 500€ or as simple as 10€, your donation makes our next adventure possible and brings it right to your homely screen!

I know we aren't too big of a crowd yet, but I also know that every single one of you can make one hell of a difference in your’s, as well as in other people's lives!






So, please share this message, give some money and we will finally move on towards North, by May!


Thank you all so much!

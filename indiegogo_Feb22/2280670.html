Overview
Meraki: Exchange Point is a community-based English learning center. We will offer innovative English classes in the back half of the building and a laid-back cafe in the front half. Our cafe will serve a variety of high-quality coffees and teas and will be open to the whole community, whether you are enrolled in our innovative English classes or not. We envision Meraki as a community center and a comfortable place to discuss new perspectives and exchange culture.

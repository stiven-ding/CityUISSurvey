Sherman Duke Buys the Farm - A student film




Story

Overview
Sherman Duke Buys the Farm is about a veterinarian who's job has devolved into nothing but putting animals to sleep, in a universe where euphemisms for death are literal. This film is being produced by a small group of students for their Senior project. This story means a lot to us, and we'd love to be able to produce it!

Short Summary

  • Hannah Gentry (Producer) and Hannah Beal (Director)- film students just trying to create work we're proud of.
  • Sherman Duke Buys the Farm is a film about the journey from life to death, told through an older man who's lost everything that ever mattered to him. This film explores the human fear of death and the eventual acceptance of mortality.
  • By contributing to this campaign, you'll be helping us afford sets and props, as well as provide a group of students with the means to make a film they're pouring their hearts and souls into.

 

What We Need

  • We're asking for $1000 for this project. This money will be used to purchase props, costuming, pay for set rental, travel expenses, and lunch during shooting. Every little bit helps!

The Impact

  • Sherman Duke Buys the Farm is a film that will contribute to the visibility of the LGBT community.
  • This film will build the portfolios of everyone on crew, and will be submitted to film festivals.

Risks & Challenges

Filmmaking is never a walk in the park- we are prepared for anything to go wrong at any given moment. We always have a backup, whether it be for locations, actors, crew members, or even equipment. You'll have to kill us to get us off track!

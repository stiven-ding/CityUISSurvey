Summary

Hello and greetings from Southern California. I am Dr. Moses Seenarine, teacher, author and publisher. Plant-based diets can stop climate chaos! And we are on a mission to prove this! 

Please join us in spreading the plant-based revolution to a healthier and greener future.

What We Need & What You Get

To promote the plant-based solution to climate change, we are launching a must-read, essential guidebook on the intersection of climate and diet - "Meat Climate Change: The 2nd Leading Cause of Global Warming." 

This book would be useful to anyone interested in learning about climate change, the environment, diet and health, social inequality, and animal-based agribusiness. 

We are reaching out to like minded individuals who can help us with the book launch. We are trying to raise $2,000 to pay for marketing, promotional materials, and a book tour in California. 

You get lots of perks for your generous contributions, from book recommendations and digital posters, to digital and printed copies of the book.

The Impact

Meat Climate Change is one of the most relevant books of our times, and a blueprint for human survival. Climate warming is a clear and present danger, and food is the second leading cause, next to fossil fuel. 

To slow the pace of global warming, it is vital to reduce fossil fuels, use renewable energy, and practice energy conservation. But this is not enough. Reducing animal consumption is critical, and this publication shows how to get us there.

The funds raised will be used to reach out to the general public, students, educators, social and environmental activists, climate scientists and policy-makers. We have three conferences scheduled so far and adding more talks, book signing, and other outreach events for the year.

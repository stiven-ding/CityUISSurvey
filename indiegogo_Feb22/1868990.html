Overview
O-stick : A stick based controller, is a combination of simple and innovative way of interacting with virtual reality content which aims to seamlessly blend between real and virtual world by providing users with a simple & familiar stick like device and comfortable head gears Key features : 1. Works on All Mobile Powered VR headset 2. Works on Google cardboard 3. Swipe and get select option in VR Technical requirements * Smartphones must have gyro sensor and compass/magnetometer

What is O-stick 

O-stick is basically a combination of simple and innovative way of interacting with virtual reality content 

It is  a controller which Replaces convention google cardboard magnet and gave you an innovative way of interaction with VR world 
O-stick based controller that we have made is a simple but an innovative approach towards making VR accessories more handy and useful , which enhances user experience by providing them a magic wand in their hand

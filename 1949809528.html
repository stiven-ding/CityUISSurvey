 In this dystopian future, not only are ghosts real, but they are a real problem. In order to combat the increasing threats of the paranormal, a mysterious and powerful organization was formed called the Paramedium Guild. While the Guild's invention and control of phantom technology makes it a strong political and militarily power, the Guild has an even greater weapon, the Paramedium Cleric.

The Paramedium Cleric is an enforcement officer of the Guild, highly trained in weapon arts, close-quarters combat, and psycho defense. Their job is to assess and resolve paranormal threats peacefully if possible, but if necessary, by force.

Depending on a Cleric's skill and experience, they are Ranked C, B, A or S, with S being the highest rank and authorized to take on the highest threat level assignments. Assignments are given by the Paramedium Guild and ranked in threat levels from 1-99 depending on the predicted danger of the offending target(s).

Will you have what it takes to be a Paramedium Cleric?

Take on the role of a Paramedium Cleric and dispense justice to otherworldly outlaws. Explore locations, investigate crimes, solve puzzles, and defend yourself against things that go bump in the night. However, you may find that the living can be just as dangerous as the dead...

 Gameplay will be broken up into several categories: story, exploration, upgrading, and combat. Dialogue and character interactions will be presented in the character sprite and text box format of the original Paramedium demos. However, we want the full game to have a more interactive storytelling style by adding an in-depth exploration feature to each assignment. So now you will be freely walking from room to room, finding clues and solving mysteries.

   While you may be forced to defend yourself often during your investigation of a location, you can expect a much larger threat (boss fight) at the end of each assignment. However, before that boss fight, if you have been diligently investigating, you will have a chance to upgrade an aspect of your Cleric and weapon.

Like the original Paramedium demos, combat will be high paced and vary depending on the nature of the assignment. However, you can still expect a face-to-face confrontation with your greatest fears!

All of our Handpainted Backgrounds are created with care. We will be creating a lot more haunting interactive spooky environment for you to explore in the game!

Story

As adults it is extremely hard to change our ways and develop a good habit. You would all agree when we say that it is much easier to pick up good habits as a kid. 

Have it Habits is a superhero game for kids that teaches important habits at an early age. It’s a first-of-its-kind game with a blend of technology and application in real life. The game is designed to make the process of learning new habits easy and fun for both parents and kids.   

We all recognize the need of good habits because they impact every aspect of our lives. Today, the impact of lack of habits are seen in the form of global warming, climate change and plastic usage among many other serious issues.  We believe big changes start with small habits. 

Instilling good habits in kids today can change the world tomorrow.

We have built a prototype of the game to show it's functionality. You can try it out here. The current graphics is just to support it. The game will be revamped with new UI that will consist of new city maps, superheroes and monsters and a better habit land. With the limited resources this is our best foot forward. 

  Every parent is busy in one way or another. While some of us are parents ourselves others have seen our parents work hard in what they do. The Parent Panel is designed keeping in mind simplicity and usage. It teaches kids good habits and makes your life easier as well. You can see your child's progress as and when they play and award them for performing habits in real life. Add new modules to the game that you want the kids to learn and see them play and perform.

 Kids need to learn and unlearn in today's world. The game is designed to teach kids in a fun manner so that they enjoy learning habits in the form of stories, videos and content. Learning is the first stepping stone in order to making habits a muscle memory in their brain.  

 Kids love to play and  we have all been there. Be it our favourite Super Mario or Tetris, if they engage you, you would go on and on until the day ends. Our game is a blend of strategy and game theory which will want kids to learn habits for them to progress in the game. With over 25 cities to strategize and saving it from the monsters, your kids wouldn't want to stop and for the first time in the history, you wouldn't want them to stop either. In order to save everyone from that disaster, we have limited the playing time per day which can be adjusted in the parental panel.

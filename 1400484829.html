What Is Puzzometry?

Puzzometry is a cool and unique kind of puzzle made from laser-cut 0.22" thick acrylic.  There is no picture on the puzzle as you would find on a jigsaw puzzle.  The concept is a simple one: can you fit all of pieces into the frame?  Trust me, it's much more difficult than it seems!   If you're thinking Puzzometry provides a challlenge similar to a jigsaw puzzle, think again!  These puzzles are more of a brainteaser along the lines of the various mechanical games and logic games.  There are literally thousands of possibilities! Can you find the correct one to solve Puzzometry?

With your help, hopefully this project will allow me to cover the initial setup expenses  involved with the design and testing of these new puzzles!

The Newest Members of the Puzzometry Family

The three new puzzles are called:  "Puzzometry Steps", "Puzzometry Six" and "Puzzometry Jr 2".

These new puzzles are the same physical size and share the same aesthetic look as the previous Puzzometry Puzzles, but they are based on a new set of geometric patterns.  

"Puzzometry Steps":  All of the pieces of this puzzle are based on a tessellation of  a "plus sign" shape as you can see in the image below.  Puzzometry Steps has 13 pieces. 

"Puzzometry Six":  All of the pieces of Puzzometry Six are based on a tessellation of slightly squished hexagons as shown in the below image.  Puzzometry Six has 14 pieces.

"Puzzometry Jr 2":  This puzzle has 7 pieces just like the original Puzzometry Jr, but all of its pieces are from a subset of the 13 pieces of Puzzometry Steps.   Don't be surprised if these 7 pieces provide a bigger challenge than you might guess!

The Other Members Of The Puzzometry Family

There are 4 existing members of the Puzzometry family.  One of the backer levels on this project will allow you to get the entire family of 7 puzzles!

- Puzzometry (Original): This is the original 14 piece brainteaser based on a tessellation of octagons and tiny squares!  A beautiful and challenging introduction to the Puzzometry Product Line

- Puzzometry Squares: This puzzle also has 14 pieces and all of the shapes are based on a tessellation of square and rectangular patterns!

- Puzzometry Jr: This puzzle is made of a selection of 7 of the 14 pieces from the Original Puzzometry.  Only 7 pieces, but don't underestimate the challenge of this one!

- Puzzometry Hex: This is the fourth member of the family.  It's based on a tessellation of hexagon shapes and it was launched as a Kickstarter Project in 2014.  This puzzle has 12 pieces and has multiple solutions to make the difficulty a bit more....well, a bit less challenging!  However, trust me...it's still  a challenge!

2-Player Strategy Game

With the exception of the two Jr puzzles, all of the Puzzometry puzzles also can be used for a two-player strategy game.  When playing the two-person strategy game (Game Mode), Puzzometry is a fast-paced, fun strategy game that requires thinking ahead and playing defense! Players alternate putting pieces into the frame with the goal being to leave not enough remaining space in the frame for your opponent to play his or her next piece!

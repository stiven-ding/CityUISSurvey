Story

 Q: Is the game really ready to go to print? 

A: Of course! We have said many times that this is our model. We want to ensure that there are as few issues and delays as possible. The game has been tested, art has been completed, and files are ready to send to the printer.

Q: Card Sizes?

A: The cards are poker sized and will use standard sized sleeves.

Q: What about Stretch Goals?

A: Excellent question! There is the issue of Stretch Goals. They may add on a week or two per stretch goal while the art is being created. This may not be much of an issue because our artist is not only extremely talented, but fast! That being said, the ship date has been set to include some Stretch Goals.

Q: Pledge Manager Access?

A: It is my goal to have all pledge levels gain access to the Pledge Manager. 

Q: How replayable is the game?

A: With the current 6 (7 with the promo) Overlords, and only using at most 3 at a time, there is a high level of replayability. In addition, each new Band opens up new strategies and play-styles and new Demons offer new challenges. 

Risks and challenges

The most common risks with any project are those of shipping and delays. We have made a promise at Laughing Rogue that we will never go to Kickstarter unless a project is ready to go to print. We have planned our timeline to include an extra month or so for any unforeseen delays with manufacturing or shipping.

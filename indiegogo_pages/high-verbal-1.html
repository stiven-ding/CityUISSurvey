MOONRAKERS: The Future-forward Vegan Leather Coat




Story

It’s a new era in fashion innovation -- the first highly functional, durable vegan leather. A vintage look and feel, from materials created to last well into the future. Function meets fashion in the timeless MOONRAKERS Vegan Leather Coat.

It’s Japanese innovation & craftsmanship, the precision-designed fabric that is made to last. Reimagine leather with NANODESIGN™ technology, combining both comfort of natural materials and the functionality of synthetics. MOONRAKER's 0.3mm ultra-thin fabric made from 0.19 decitex ultrafine fibers. The innovative design of fabric cross section and special yarn structure provides the strength and feel of leather at ultra-lightweight. The new era vegan leather with innovative design and texture brings whole new feel and one-of-a-kind style. Artisanal “garment dyeing” by Japanese craftsmen gives your MOONRAKERS a unique color and vintage, naturally worn look that styles your way. This hand-dyed garment’s tasteful vintage-like color elevates any outfit from sweaters to T-shirts. The high-density weave of the fabric is wind resistant and weather-proof with high water repellency. It allows for an air permeability of 0.33cc or lees, perfect for transitioning through the seasons. You can wear this very unconventional coat in fall, winter and spring even in the hardcore outdoor weather and damp conditions. As we innovate with fabrics, we weave in traditional craftsmanship by delicately hand-dying each coat, so no two colors are exactly alike. But every coat we craft is ultra-light, weather-proof, and versatile throughout the year.

Our ultra-thin NANODESIGN™ fabrics provide all the comfort and qualities of traditional leather while offering high functionality for weatherproof. We continually research and innovate the creation of fabrics that provide the comfort and familiarity of natural materials and allow us to go beyond conventional functions and textures. Like leather, but better. Toray's NANODESIGN™ Technology gives you all the feel without all the weight.

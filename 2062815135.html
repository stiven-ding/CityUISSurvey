HOW DO I PLAY?

Check out the instructions: View that Shit! But here is a brief explanation of the cards and how they work.

  •  Weapon Cards - These are the objects of your awesomeness! As you go through the storyteller's adventure you'll smash all sorts of things that will give you loot.  Sometimes the loots are mods, these mods add bonuses to your weapon that allow you smash even better than before!
  •  Smash that Shit! Cards - These can be anything from story arcs, quests, monsters, npcs, or objects that serve no greater purpose than to be smashed! After they get their asses kicked, they join your entourage.  Sacrifice them to other opponents to smash even harder badies or keep them around for extra points at the end of the game.  Whenever you smash a Smash that Shit! Card you get loot - as we all know, loot is sexy.
  •  Keep that Shit! Cards - The more loot you have, the more people admire your badassery.  Loot can help you fulfill quests, provide mods for your weapon, or allow you to gloat to your friends about how your treasure hoard is bigger. The more Keep that Shit! cards you have at the end of the game gives you more sexy points. 
  •  Boss Cards - There's always some bastard at the end of the adventure who needs to be taught a lesson.  One of these cards is drawn during the final battle, where it becomes a free-for-all Smash fest.  Each time you smash the boss you get a heart, the more hearts you get, the more points you win!

What is The Silver Key Lounge?

We are a board game bar focused on serving craft beer and wine with plans to open our doors in Mesa, Arizona on or around October 2019. With your help and a small pledge we plan to make that opening a night to remember!  

The lease has been signed and keys are in hand. We will begin the build out process soon with a regigstered contractor ROC# 257087 with doors opening in October of 2019 barring techincal issues.

Games

While our game library is relatively extensive, we are continually expanding, backing vetted Kickstarters, searching small publishers and attending conferences to ensure we can offer not only the latest and greatest but those hard to find gems for years to come. We take this process to heart and what to provide our patrons with the best we can offer! 

Buildout

We plan to offer a themed and immersive experience for our customers.We didn't want to just be another pub! Our patrons will enjoy themed décor and cosmetic upgrades, well laid out space and and an amazing hand finished bar all promoting a fun speak easy feel.

A huge game library.

 For a variable $0 to $10 table fee (based on availability), choose from our library of over 700 planned board game titles  for our patrons to play while enjoying their drinks. Ranging from simple and quick card games to your favorite multi-hour grind session games! With booths and table space available you can set up your games in style and comfort.  We will also showcase four 4.6 Elite Ultimate Game Tables for the large game groups along with our standard 3x3 and 3x5 tables to sit at comfortably tabletop wargames are welcome!!! 

Live Streaming Table. 

Live streaming games and podcasts is becoming more and more popular. We at the Silver Key Lounge want our guests to get in on the action and will offer the use of a Live Stream Table in the bar itself! Nothing like enjoying a brew or two and recording the action to post on your favorite feeds or just as a memory keepsake.If you want to Live Stream gameplay or Vlog, rent out our Elite 4.6 Table of Ultimate Live Streaming for a few hours. The rental fee is $5 an hour. We supply the cameras, lights, and sound. Just plug your laptop in and you should be up and streaming within a few minutes.

Game Specialists. Want help learning a new game? An experienced game specialist is here to help you get set up and get playing faster. Our staff will be trained to play the hottest titles as well as the popular classics and will attend training to grow that knowledge consistently! 

Drinks & More Drinks. 

We plan to serve 16 craft beers on draft alone, along with over 20 other craft canned/bottled beers which will be rotated as season and customer requests change! For our patrons who prefer a glass of wine we will maintain an extensive wine list consisting of local and international varietals to choose from along with our 4 house wines. Bottle service will be available. If you are a non drinker we will offer a great selection of craft soda and coffee (for those late night gaming sessions!). 

Special events. Join us for a variety of themed game nights and events. You can even book your own fully catered private parties and events. Events include tabletop wargame  tournaments, Magic the Gathering Tournaments, Keyforge Tournaments, etc. Costum and cosplay contests, E sports viewing events, date nights and more! We will also hold special Beer and Wine tasting events each month and of course special events for holidays to boot.

The "Store". Pick up a board game or choose from a variety of third party accessories, Kickstarter Exclusive games, Silver Key Lounge merchandise, and specialty items that are theme or game related. 

Private Rooms.  The Silver Key Lounge with host two private rooms to reserve in six hour blocks that hold you and a select number of guests for parties, rpg sessions, board games birthdays and more. The fee is $40 for the block per room based on demand. Fees will be waved if sales exceed the rental fee. Block Times are 12 noon to 6pm and 7pm to 1am seven days a week. 

Location. The Silver Key Lounge is located at 1837 W. Guadalupe Road, Mesa, AZ 85202.

Risks and challenges

This business has everything it needs to open, but the funds from Kickstarter will allow us to help make certain upgrades to the location like more Tables of Ultimate Gaming for players to use as well as throw a bigger Grand Opening celebration and add more to the game library to keep it expanding. Opening a business is never easy and sometimes there are road blocks that can slow the process down. We will make sure to keep everyone updated if we hit said road block and have to extend the opening date a few weeks or so.
